This addon extends the mod [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2258619623]Berlin Stadtbahn Viaduct Construction - Basic Segmets[/url] and adds the following features:

[list]
    [*]5 meter wide block with brick texture (in addition to the block with concrete texture)
    [*]Brick wall railing (in addition to concrete wall, railing, none)
    [*]Both can be combined with all the other assets
    [*]Height is now infinite by default (was 8 previously)
[/list]
